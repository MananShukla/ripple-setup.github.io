Step 4: Connect to Ripple Health Wi-Fi

 Go to your Settings App, and access the Ripple Health Wi-Fi. 

Please look at video before attempting!

 

This step is very important.

Please read the following beforehand.

If you need additional help, select your device type, and watch the given video. 

1. Close out of this browser.

2. Go to your Settings App. 

3. Tap on "Wi-Fi"

4. Wait until you find a network called

"Ripple Health"

5. Click on "Ripple Health". 

6. If you have done the following

correctly, you have set up the pill bottle

successfully!
